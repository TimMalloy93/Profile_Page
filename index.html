About me

I have over a decade of experience in retail, where I’ve held various positions at my previous company, Circle Liquor Store. I started as an entry-level stocker and progressed through roles such as cashier, customer support, and wine manager.

In customer support I learned the importance of how to effectively interface with customers and help them have a positive experience. As a manager, in addition to working with customers I now also was balancing my time with sales reps and was in charge of a team of 12 where I needed to illustrate my ability to be a team leader.

This is how my part-time college job turned into a career due to my drive to learn and do more. I aimed to understand the products I worked with, regardless of my role, and consistently took the initiative to learn and take on additional tasks.

As I advanced in the business, I became the in-house IT support for hardware and software issues. For more complex issues, I worked with our POS provider for troubleshooting. These experiences made me recognize my passion for technology. Learning about Tech Elevator from my brother, I decided to make a career change. I applied the same diligence to my studies, spending extra time practicing and applying new concepts. Considering my background in retail and my commitment to excellence, I believe I can be a valuable member of your team.
